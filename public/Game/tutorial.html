How to Play

Step 1: Funding Your Account
  • Top up your in game wallet in the store page
  • Choose from the 3 funding options
  • Follow the metamask prompt to fund your wallet
Step 2: Buying your Drivers
  • Go to the Driver Market
  • Choose and purchase your preferred 2 Drivers
Step 3: Buying your Cars
  • Go to the Car Market
  • Choose and purchase your preferred 2 Cars
Step 4: Drafting your Team
  • Proceed to the Draft page after starting a race
  • Allocate 1 Driver and 1 Car to each of your
    2 race pairs
Step 5:Select your Config
  • Proceed to the Config page after starting a race
  • Choose one of the 3 configs based on
    the race conditions
Step 6:Results
  • View results and strategise your next race!
